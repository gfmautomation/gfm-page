Our Services

At GFM Automation, we offer a comprehensive range of services designed to transform your business operations. From AI-powered customer service solutions to advanced data analysis, we help businesses streamline their processes and boost efficiency. One of our key offerings is Adapt Apps Form Building, where we create powerful forms and administration systems that integrate seamlessly with your existing workflows. These custom forms help eliminate manual data entry, reduce errors, and provide better data management capabilities for your business.

Our Adapt Apps solutions are particularly valuable for businesses looking to modernize their administrative processes. Whether you need customer intake forms, internal approval workflows, or quoting and invoicing systems, we can build forms that not only capture the information you need but also integrate with your existing software stack to create a truly automated workflow.
